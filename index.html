Professional Architect & Artist

I'm yustina Nageeb Naeem, an architect graduated from the Faculty of Fine Arts, Architecture Department. My journey began at the Industrial Technical Institute, Architecture Department, then I developed myself further by joining the Faculty of Fine Arts to pursue my passion for design and creativity.

What distinguishes me is that my start at the technical institute gave me practical experience and my college developed artistic sense and creativity by combining engineering accuracy with artistic vision. I always love to add a distinctive artistic touch in every work I present, whether it's an architectural project or a painted artwork.
